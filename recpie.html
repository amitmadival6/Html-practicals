SPICY CHICKEN TIKKA

Chicken Tikka is a popular Indian dish made from marinated chicken pieces that are grilled or baked to perfection. It's known for its vibrant color and rich flavors, often served with mint chutney and naan bread.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, garlic, ginger, and salt to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers.
  5. Grill or bake the chicken for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with mint chutney and naan bread.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Chicken Tikka!
